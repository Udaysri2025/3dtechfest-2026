---

## ✨ Features

- **Scroll‑Driven Camera** – Seamlessly orbit around a futuristic 3D scene by scrolling.
- **5 Snap‑Scroll Sections** – Home, About, Events, Speakers, Register – each with unique 3D visuals.
- **Interactive Objects** – Click on the central torus knot, particles, or section‑specific shapes to trigger bursts and shockwaves.
- **Responsive & Mobile‑Friendly** – Adapts layout, hamburger menu, and particle counts for all screen sizes.
- **Dynamic Lighting & Fog** – Colors and intensities shift as you scroll, creating an evolving atmosphere.
- **Keyboard Navigation** – Use `Arrow Keys` or `1‑4` to jump between sections.
- **Preloader Animation** – Smooth loading screen with a progress bar.
